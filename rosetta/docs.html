The Wanderer

So for our decyphering of The Wanderer we took all of the instances it is used in other symbols as well as looked at what it meaned by itself. This being:

The Pilgrim: Obtained by visiting all of the echos in a given playthough. "Listened to the conflation of echoes"
Pilgrimage: Obtained by gaining the highest karma by only visiting echos. "The highest enlightenment, achieved by the hardest path. A feat even guardians must respect."
The Scholar: Obtained by finding a data pearl and bringing it to Looks To The Moon. "History lessons."
The Wanderer: Obtained by visiting all normally accessible regions of a given slugcats campaign. "Your travels have taken you far."

When doing so we concluded that when getting all of these achievements you had to go out of the way to get them, away from the standard path which makes sense given the base achievement name is "The Wanderer". Then we looked a bit deeper and tried to find a more general meaning than just wandering, it came to me that things like the scholar must mean you have to set out to find these things, to explore to find pearls. So that is when it clicked, not all at once but it was a slow bubbling up of getting a grapple on the meaning. Eventually with a bit of diliberation it was decided that the wanderer must mean to explore, discover, embrace curiosity, and to learn, experience, and interact with the world around you. That is the Wanderer's meaning and how we found it.
